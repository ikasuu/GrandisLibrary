Introduction:

Demon Avenger is a HP warrior class part of the Demons & Resistance class group. Demon Avenger uses Desperados as primary weapons. As a secondary, Demon Avengers can equip Demon Aegis

Demon Avengers use HP for their attacks and do not have an MP resource. As well, instead of scaling off of STR like regular warriors, Demon Avengers' damage scales off of HP

For more information about Demon Avenger check out:

http://maplestory.nexon.net/game/classes-and-jobs/3371/demon-avenger

https://maplestory.fandom.com/wiki/Demon

How to create a Demon Avenger:

To create one choose the "The Demon" option in the Character Creation screen and then continue through the story until Lv. 10 where you will be given the choice to choose between the "Demon Avenger" or "Demon Slayer" classes. You can also choose to skip the cutscenes at the beginning to go straight into choosing between the two classes

Pros & Cons:

Pros:

  • Tanky class
  • Simple class
  • Only uses HP, does not consume MP
  • Has glide skill to quickly move through map
  • Has a bind with Blood Prison (4th Job)
  • Not too many buffs to manage

Cons:

  • Simple class, linear gameplay style
  • Falls off lategame because of attacks cost HP
    • And bosses deal %HP damage
  • Unconventional class compared to Stat classes (but %HP gear is cheap!)

Overall:

Demon Avenger an interesting class since they only use HP. The class is a bit too simple for me but their theme is really cool. I love the design of Demon Avenger's weapon a lot. Dimensional Sword in 5th job and Nether Shield in 4th Job are one of my favourite skills in Demon Avenger that look really cool skill and totally adds to the thematic of Demon Avenger. Try out the class, maybe you'll enjoy it!

Class Specific V Skills:

  • Demonic Frenzy [Toggle Buff]: Consumes 6000 HP per second, for every 4% HP consumed, you gain 1% Final Damage and you coat the ground with your blood which deals damage
    • When your HP falls below 1%, the buff will disappear
    • Items and skills can only recover 1% Max HP but you take reduced damage
    • Has a cooldown (149 - 120 secs depending on Skill Level)

  • Demonic Blast [Key-down Active Attack]: Charge up an attack and unleash it by releasing the Skill Key
    • When fully charged, you will restore %Max HP and the attack will have 100% Ignore DEF and is a guaranteed Critical Hit
    • Has a cooldown (10 secs)

  • Dimensional Sword [Active Attack]: Blades will hover above you while attacking nearby enemies
    • If you press the Skill Key again while the skill is active, the duration is reduced but the skill will spin around you dealing damage to enemies
    • Attack has 100% Ignore DEF
    • Has a cooldown (120 secs)

  • Revenant [Buff Attack]: While active, you cannot go below 1HP and whenever you use an Exceed attack, an additional attack appears
    • All damage taken will be stored as "Rage" (up to 200% of Max HP)
      • Rage will slowly decay while the skill is active
      • Cooldown of the additional attack is reduced based on your Rage
      • At the end of the skill, you will take damage equal to the Rage stored but it cannot kill you
    • Additional attack has increased %Ignore DEF and ignores Damage Reflect
    • Pressing the Skill Key again will cancel the skill
    • Has a cooldown (4 secs) between each additional attack and a cooldown of 300 secs
