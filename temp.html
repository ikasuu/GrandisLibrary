Introduction:

Luminous is an INT mage class part of the Heroes class group. Luminous uses Shining Rod as primary weapons. As a secondary, Luminous can equip Orbs

What is unique about Luminous is their ability to control both Light and Dark magic. The strength of Luminous' magic will shift between Light and Dark thus requiring you to alternate between both. As well, Luminous has a middle state called Equilibrium where both Light and Dark magic are at their strongest and you also gain access to special attacks with no cooldowns

Pros & Cons:

Pros:

  • Has good mobbing
  • Has a bind with Armageddon (Hyper Skill)
  • Good link skill (provides %Ignore DEF, good for bossing)
  • Simple class that has dedicated bossing and mobbing attacks

Cons:

  • Weak teleport skill but becomes incredible while in Equilibrium mode
  • Has lots of active buffs to manage

Overall:

A simple, beginner-friendly class that has pretty great mobbing. Reflection (4th Job) is really fun to use because it bounces to enemies around you. You can just stand still and just spam that skill. Ender (4th Job) is also pretty fun to use because of the way the skill strikes the enemy and deals massive amounts of damage. As well, in Equilibrium mode, you can spam Death Scythe (3rd Job) and Ender and just watch the map get destroyed. If you are looking for a simple class with an interesting light and dark mechanic, try out Luminous!

1ST JOB SKILLS:

Based on a choice you make, you can choose between Light or Dark magic. There is little difference except for a passive %Final Damage boost to either your Light or Dark magic. If you skip the beginning cutscenes, you will automatically receive the Dark magic bonuses. The %Final Damage is almost marginal but Light magic is preferred because of the attack Reflection (4th Job)

  • Abyssal Drop [Active Attack]: Drops several projectiles in front of you

    • While in Equilibrium mode, you can teleport in the air and on ladders

All Possible Boost Nodes obtainable:

V Skills:

  • Freud's Wisdom [Buff]: Provides a stacking buff with each stack providing an additional effect
    • [Stack 1]: Skill Cooldowns reduced (excluding skills unaffected by cooldown reduction)
    • [Stack 2]: Increased %Knockback Resistance
    • [Stack 3]: Increased All Stats
    • [Stack 4]: Increased Attack & Magic Attack
    • [Stack 5]: Increased %Boss Damage
    • [Stack 6]: Invincible for the duration of the skill
    • Cooldown between each stack (25 secs) and 240 sec cooldown for when buff expires or max stack count has been reached

  • Maple World Goddess Blessing [Buff]: Increases the effects of greatly and grants increased %Damage
    • Has a cooldown (180 secs)

  • Mana Overload [Toggle Buff]: When toggled, increases the %Final Damage of all your skills (excluding summons)
    • Consumes %Max MP every second when toggled
      • Consumes %Max HP for classes without MP
    • Has a cooldown (59 - 30 secs depending on Skill Level)

  • Ethereal Form [Buff]: While active, you gain 100% Knockback Resistance and lose a fixed amount of MP instead of taking damage
    • When MP is 0 you take a fixed amount of HP damage
    • Has a cooldown (60 - 53 secs depending on Skill Level)
