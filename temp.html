Introduction:

Jett is a DEX pirate class part of the Explorers class group. Jett uses Guns as primary weapons. As a secondary, Jetts can equip Fists

Although Jetts are categorized with Explorers, Jetts are not connected to Explorer classes and cannot Job Change to an Explorer Pirate. They do, however, share Cash Shop Inventories with Explorer classes meaning you can transfer items like pets and cash clothing from Explorer classes to Jetts

Pros & Cons:

Pros:

  • Great mobbing
  • Lots of innate stats from passive
  • Good mobility with Space Walk and Vortex Cross

Cons:

  • Incredibly poor bossing damage
  • Damage past Lv. 200 is poor compared to other classes
  • Lots of active buffs to maintain

Overall:

Jett is a class that has incredible mobility and great mobbing pre-200. However, past Lv. 200, Jetts fall hard in damage especially against bosses compared to other classes. Jett also does not receive many changes and is neglected by Nexon much like Beast Tamer. In terms of speaking just about pre-200 Jett, they have skills like Space Walk that allows them to easily jump from one side of the map to the other and Vortex Cross which allows them to dash both horizontally and vertically. Jetts also have a lot of strong mob destroying attacks like Cosmic Upheaval, Planet Buster, and Starfall. Overall, Jetts are good as Legion Characters as they provide %Crit Damage but other than that, it is best to stay away from this class
