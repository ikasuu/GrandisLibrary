Introduction:

Hayato is a STR warrior class part of the Sengoku. Hayato uses Katanas as primary weapons. As a secondary, Hayato can equip Kodachis

Hayato also has a secondary resource called Sword Energy which certain skills consume. With Sword Energy, you also gain bonus stats depending on the amount you have. Hayato also has two stances one for mobbing and one for bossing which you can switch between at anytime

For more information about Hayato check out:

http://maplestory.nexon.net/game/classes-and-jobs/3373/hayato

https://maplestory.fandom.com/wiki/Hayato

Pros, Cons, and Concerns:

Pros:

  • Has a strong unique basic attack
  • Very mobile class with great mobbing
  • Makes use of skills from previous Job advancements allowing for some interesting combinations
  • Ultimate skills like Falcon's Honor, Battoujutsu Zankou, Iaijutsu Phantom Blade and Battoujutsu Ultimate Will - God of Blades have relatively short cooldowns
  • Lots of bonuses from skills like %Attack, %Ignore Def, %Boss Damage, %Crit Rate and %Knockback Resistance
    • Hitokiri Strike buff grants a free 45% Crit Rate
  • Has a strong Lifesteal passive from Warrior's Heart
  • Can resist all Knockbacks using Sudden Strike to counter
  • Battoujutsu Zankou is an iFrame

Cons:

  • Certain skills can be clunky and lock you in place like Surging Blade, Vapor Blade, Hitokiri Strike and Falcon's Honor
  • Can be a lot of attacks to manage at certain points
  • Can easily die from Damage Reflect if not careful especially with skills like Quick Draw, Rai Blade Flash and Shinsoku
  • Can be a bit spammy with using Rai Sanrenzan for mobbing and Rai Blade Flash for bossing
  • In your Bossing Stance, Quick Draw, you do not have a Double Jump/Triple Jump
  • Must maintain Iaijutsu Phantom Blade buff in order to keep damage up

Overall:

I am a bit bias since this is my main and current favourite class. Jumping around and using Rai Sanrenzan and Phantom Blade for mobbing is really fun. Your bossing skills are also really cool with the swift attacking Rai Blade Flash and Battoujutsu Zankou. You also have lots of summons in 5th job with God of Blades and Sengoku Force, Assemble! Not to mention, you have LIFESTEAL!!! It is a really big deal since Hayato's lifesteal is not blocked from healing restrictions

BEGINNER SKILLS:

  • Master of Blades [Passive]: Increases Movement Speed, Max Speed Limit, Jump, %Avoidibility, Willpower trait

  • Return of the Five Planets [Active]: Returns to Momijigaoka, Hayato's hometown
    • Has a cooldown (600 secs)

  • Quick Draw [Active]: Changes stances between Normal Stance and Quick Draw stance which allows you to use different skills
    • [Normal Stance]: Works best for mobbing
      • In this stance, you gain increased %Attack, HP, MP, %Ignore Def, and %Knockback Resistance
    • [Quick Draw Stance]: Works best for single targets like bosses
      • Changing into Quick Draw stance will cost a maximum of 100 Sword Energy (check below for info about Sword Energy)
      • In this stance, you gain increased %Boss Damage, Attack Speed (by 1 stage), %Stun chance, %Crit Rate and %Knockback Resistance
      • In 3rd job, you gain a damaging aura around you
        • Be careful, this aura can trigger Damage Reflect!
    • [Note]: Skills marked on this post with a can only be used in Normal Stance and skills marked with can only be used in Quick Draw stance

  • Summer Rain [ Active Attack]: Unleashes your ultimate attack
    • Upon using this skill, you will gain a buff that increases your %Damage
    • Can be used only when you have max Sword Energy and will consume it all

  • Hitokiri Hundred Strike [ Active Attack]: Unleashes your ultimate attack
    • Upon using this skill, you will gain a buff that increases your %Damage

Sword Energy - Secondary Resource:

Sword Energy is Hayato's secondary resource that some skills consume. Although, the main benefit is that as Hayato charges up Sword Energy, his Stance bonuses become stronger

  • Killing enemies in this stance grants 5 Sword Energy
  • Each attack in this stance grants 2 Sword Energy

These skills below generate Sword Energy when used:

  • Battoujutsu Advance (1st Job)
  • Falcon's Honor (Hyper Skill)
  • Battoujutsu Zankou (5th Job)
  • Battoujutsu Ultimate Will - God of Blades (5th Job)

These skills cost Sword Energy to use:

  • Quick Draw (Beginner) - Costs a maximum of 100 Sword Energy, can be used when you have less than 100 Sword Energy
  • Iaijutsu Phantom Blade (5th Job)
  • Battoujutsu Ultimate Will - God of Blades (5th Job)

    • Shares the same Key Slot with Battoujutsu Dash

    • Can be assigned a Key Slot or activated by pressing the Arrow Key of the direction you want to go twice (Left or Right)
    • Shares the same Key Slot with Battoujutsu Leap

  • Battoujutsu Advance [Key-down Special Active]: A special skill that unleashes your Battoujutsu attacks back to back
    • To be used properly, you must not move and the skill must be assigned to a Key Slot
    • Grants a stacking %Attack buff and restores 8 Sword Energy for each Battoujutsu attack used
