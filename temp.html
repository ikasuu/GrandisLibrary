Introduction:

Paladin is a STR warrior class part of the Explorers. Paladins use Swords and Blunt Weapons as primary weapons. As a secondary, Paladins can equip Shields or Rosaries

For more information about Paladin check out: http://maplestory.nexon.net/game/classes-and-jobs/3376/warrior https://maplestory.fandom.com/wiki/Warrior/Skills https://dexless.com/guides/an-excessively-in-depth-guide-to-everything-paladin.12/

How to create a Paladin:

To create one, choose the "Explorers" option in the Character Creation screen and then continue through the story until you reach the quest "Victoria Island or Bust" and choose the "Warrior, powerful and defensive" option

Once you reach Lv. 30 as a Warrior, talk to Dances with Balrog to accept a quest called "Way of the Page". Completing the quest will make you a Page and you will be on the path to a Paladin. Page is the 2nd job of Paladin

Pros & Cons:

Pros:

  • Well-rounded
  • Tanky
  • Support
  • Decent mobbing
  • Lots of skills that grant invincibility
  • 100% Knockback resistance
  • Has a bind skill

Cons:

  • Short-range
  • Lack of mobility

Overall:

A simple beginner-friendly class that provides a lot of utility and is an absolute tank! The Elemental Charge skills are really cool and flashy. His 5th job skills are really cool and unique too with the ability to duplicate your attacks on a party member

Link Skill:

  • [Master Level 6] Invincible Belief [Passive]: Automatically activates when your HP falls below 15% to recover some Max HP every few seconds.
    • Can stack up to 3 times for each unique Explorer Warrior class character
      • Each Link Skill can only go up to Level 2 (stacking for a total of Skill Level 6)
    • Has a cooldown (410 - 210 secs depending on Skill Level)

1ST JOB SKILLS:

  • Slash Blast [Active Attack]: Slashes forward dealing damage to multiple enemies in front of you

  • War Leap [Active]: Double jump skill
    • Can be assigned a Key Slot or activated by pressing jump twice
  • Iron Body [Passive]: Increases %Max HP and %Damage Reduction and grants flat DEF
  • Warrior Mastery [Passive]: Increases Movement Speed, Jump, Max HP, and Knockback resistance

2ND JOB SKILLS:

  • Flame Charge [Active Attack]: Attack that deals fire damage and inflicts a damaging burn debuff

  • Blizzard Charge [Active Attack]: Attack that deals ice damage and slows enemies
    • Attacking enemies taking burn damage will deal more damage
  • Elemental Charge [Passive]: Using a different Charge skill right after another will grant 1 Elemental Charge (up to 5)
    • Each stack increases %Recovery amount, %Abnormal Status Resistance, and %Damage Reduction and grants flat Attack
      • Damage Reduction also reduces %Max HP attacks
    • Paladin has four Charge skills:
      • Flame Charge (2nd Job)
      • Blizzard Charge (2nd Job)
      • Lightning Charge (3rd Job)
      • Divine Charge (4th Job)

  • Close Combat [Active Attack]: Pulls in enemies from both sides of you
    • Attack has a chance to stun

  • Weapon Booster [Buff]: Increases Attack Speed (by 2 stages)
  • Weapon Mastery [Passive]: Stabilizes your damage range by increasing your minimum damage range
  • Final Attack [Passive]: Grants a chance to activate an additional hit after an attack
  • Physical Training [Passive]: Grants flat STR and DEX

3RD JOB SKILLS:

  • Lightning Charge [Active Attack]: Attack that deals lightning damage and stuns enemies
    • Attacking slowed enemies will deal more damage

  • HP Recovery [Active Heal]: Instantly recovers HP
    • Recovers less HP when used repeatedly

  • Rush [Active Attack]: Dash forward while pushing enemies back

  • Threaten [Active Debuff]: Debuffs enemies by reducing their %Attack, %DEF and %Accuracy

  • Parashock Guard [Toggle Buff]: Reduces your %Guard Chance and %DEF but increases your Attack
    • Also reduces damage taken by nearby party members and grants your party members a chance to guard themselves

  • Combat Orders [Party Buff]: Temporarily grants bonus skill levels to skills of all party members
    • [Passive]: Permanently increases %Damage for and
    • Exception: Beginner skills, Combat Orders, Hyper Skills and 5th Job Skills
  • Shield Mastery [Passive]: When a Shield or Rosary is equipped, increases %Elemental Resistance, %Abnormal Status Resistance, and %Defense and grants flat Attack
    • Additionally, grants a chance to block and stun enemies when Shield or Rosary is equipped
  • Achilles [Passive]: Grants %Damage Reduction
  • Divine Shield [Passive]: Creates a shield when hit that absorbs attacks and increases your Attack
    • For attacks that deal Max HP damage, reduces amount taken by a percentage
    • Has a cooldown (30 secs) after shield breaks

4TH JOB SKILLS:

  • Blast [Active Attack]: Deals a powerful attack against one enemy while applying the attribute of the most recently used Charge Skill

  • Divine Charge [Active Attack]: Attack that deals holy damage and silences enemies
    • Attacking stunned enemies will deal more damage

  • Magic Crash [Active Debuff]: Removes buffs on nearby enemies and prevents them from casting new buffs
    • Has a cooldown (60 secs)

  • Heaven's Hammer [Active Attack]: Slams your hammer dealing damage to enemies in an area
    • You are not affected by knockbacks while casting
    • Has a cooldown (30 - 15 secs depending on Skill Level)

  • Elemental Force [Buff]: Increases %Final Damage
    • [Passive]: Permanently increases %Damage of , , and
  • Power Stance [Passive]: Increases %Knockback Resistance

  • Maple Warrior [Party Buff]: Increases all stats assigned APs by a percentage
  • Hero's Will [Buff]: Removes certain abnormal status effects and become immune to Abnormal Status for a few seconds
    • Has a cooldown (600 - 360 secs depending on Skill Level)

  • Guardian [Active]: Revives one party member closest to you and makes both you and your party member invulnerable for a short time
    • Has a cooldown (1000 - 600 secs depending on Skill Level)
  • High Paladin [Passive]: Increases %Crit Rate, %Crit Damage, %Ignore DEF, and %Final Damage and grants flat DEF
    • Additionally stabilizes your damage range by increasing your minimum damage range
    • Extra bonuses depending on weapon equipped
      • %Critical Damage, %Ignore DEF and %Weapon Mastery
  • Advanced Charge [Passive]: For each charge of , increases %Damage and grants flat Attack
    • Additionally increases the max number of enemies hit and number of hits per attack of and Charge Skills

V Skills:

  • Blitz Shield [Buff]: Creates a shield based on your %Max HP that blocks damage

    • Has a cooldown (180 secs)

  • Impenetrable Skin [Buff]: While active you gain Abnormal Status Resistance and 100% Knockback Resistance. As well, when you are hit you gain a stacking %Damage buff
    • [Passive]: Permanently increases STR and Max HP

Class Specific V Skills:

  • Divine Echo [Buff Attack]: Targets a party member in range who will duplicate any attack skills and certain buffs you use.
    • Increases %Final Damage when Divine Echo is active
    • Buffs gained from Divine Echo have a shorter duration
      • The duration is based on a fixed amount + 1% for every 1000 STR up to 100%
    • Has a cooldown (120 secs)

  • Hammers of the Righteous [Active + Passive Attack]: Passively creates a hammer that circles around you when you use an Elemental Charge attack up to 5 hammers. Hammers automatically hit enemies and will gain attack speed based on the number of hammers active
      • [Active]: Hammers grow in size and spin faster while dealing more damage
        • Has a cooldown (60 secs)

  • Grand Guardian [Key-down Active Attack]: When held, deals damage and becomes bigger after some time passes which will increase attack speed and %Damage
    • Consumes %Max HP while casting
    • Attack has 100% Ignore DEF and is a guaranteed Critical Hit
      • Additionally increases your %Damage Reduction while casting, including %Max HP attacks
    • Has a cooldown (180 secs)
